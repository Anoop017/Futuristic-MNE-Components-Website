About MNE Components India

Leading manufacturer of precision-engineered battery connector solutions

Our Company

MNE Components India Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Nashik, Maharashtra, is a reputed manufacturer of EV battery pack components, including cell-to-cell interconnects, busbars, and sub-assemblies.

We provide end-to-end engineered solutions in Copper, Aluminum, Clad Metals, and Stainless Steel to serve automotive OEMs, Energy Storage Systems, and switchgear manufacturers.

We are committed to delivering innovation, reliability, and consistent quality from initial prototypes to full-scale production.
